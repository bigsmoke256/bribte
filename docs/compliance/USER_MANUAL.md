## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Accessing the System

Open your web browser and navigate to your institution's BRIBTE portal URL (e.g., `https://bribte.lovable.app` or your custom domain).

**Supported Browsers:**
- Google Chrome 90+ (recommended)
- Mozilla Firefox 90+
- Microsoft Edge 90+
- Safari 15+

### 1.2 Student Registration

1. Click **"Sign Up"** on the login page
2. Enter your **full name**, **email address**, and choose a **password**
3. Check your email inbox for a **verification link**
4. Click the verification link to confirm your account
5. Log in with your email and password
6. Your account starts with **"Pending"** status — wait for an administrator to approve and assign your course

### 1.3 Staff Login

Lecturers and administrators receive their credentials from the system administrator. Use the provided email and password to log in at the portal.

### 1.4 First Login

After logging in for the first time, we recommend:
1. Navigate to **Profile Settings** (click your avatar → Profile)
2. Update your **phone number**
3. Upload a **profile photo**
4. Review your assigned **role and permissions**

## 2. Student Guide

### 2.1 Dashboard

Your dashboard shows at a glance:
- **Fee balance** — Current outstanding amount
- **Enrolled courses** — Active course registrations
- **Upcoming assignments** — Deadlines approaching
- **Recent announcements** — Notices from admin and lecturers

### 2.2 Viewing Your Courses

1. Navigate to **Courses** from the sidebar
2. View your enrolled courses with details:
   - Course name and code
   - Lecturer name
   - Module breakdown
   - Course materials (downloadable)

### 2.3 Fee Management

#### Viewing Your Fee Breakdown

1. Navigate to **Fees** from the sidebar
2. View your complete fee structure:
   - **Tuition** — Based on your study mode (Day/Evening/Weekend)
   - **Mandatory fees** — Registration, examination, etc.
   - **Optional fees** — Select/deselect as needed
   - **Total balance** — Outstanding amount after payments

#### Uploading a Payment Receipt

1. Navigate to **Fees** from the sidebar
2. Click **"Upload Receipt"**
3. Select your payment receipt image (JPEG, PNG, or PDF)
4. The system will automatically:
   - Extract payment details using AI
   - Verify the receipt is genuine (SchoolPay format)
   - Match your name and course
   - Check for duplicate submissions
5. Status updates:
   - **✅ Verified** — Payment auto-approved, balance updated
   - **⏳ Review Required** — Flagged for admin review (minor discrepancy)
   - **❌ Rejected** — Receipt not accepted (see reason in notes)

> **Tip:** Ensure your receipt is a clear, unedited SchoolPay receipt for fastest processing.

### 2.4 Assignments

1. Navigate to **Assignments** from the sidebar
2. View all assignments with deadlines
3. Click an assignment to see instructions
4. Upload your submission file before the deadline
5. Check back later for your **grade and feedback**

### 2.5 Exam Card

1. Navigate to **Exam Card** from the sidebar
2. View your exam schedule:
   - Exam date, time, and venue
   - Course and exam type
3. Your exam card is available when you have **no outstanding fee balance**

### 2.6 Results

1. Navigate to **Results** from the sidebar
2. View your exam results by semester:
   - Marks obtained
   - Grade and grade points
   - Lecturer remarks

### 2.7 Clearance

When it's time for end-of-semester or graduation clearance:

1. Navigate to **Clearance** from the sidebar
2. Click **"Submit Clearance Request"**
3. Select the clearance type:
   - **End of Semester** — Regular semester clearance
   - **Graduation** — Final graduation clearance
4. Enter the academic year and semester
5. Click **Submit**
6. Track your 4-step approval progress:
   - Step 1: **Finance Office** — Verifies fee payment
   - Step 2: **Library** — Verifies no outstanding books
   - Step 3: **Department Head** — Academic clearance
   - Step 4: **Final Admin Approval** — Administrative sign-off

### 2.8 Timetable & Schedule

1. Navigate to **Timetable** from the sidebar
2. View your weekly class schedule:
   - Day, time, room/venue
   - Lecturer name
   - Course module

## 3. Lecturer Guide

### 3.1 Dashboard

Your lecturer dashboard shows:
- **Assigned courses** — Courses you're teaching
- **Pending submissions** — Student work awaiting review
- **Upcoming sessions** — Scheduled classes
- **Announcements** — Your posted notices

### 3.2 Managing Assignments

#### Creating an Assignment

1. Navigate to **Assignments** from the sidebar
2. Click **"Create Assignment"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** — Assignment name
   - **Course** — Select from your assigned courses
   - **Instructions** — Detailed requirements
   - **Deadline** — Due date and time
   - **Max Grade** — Maximum possible score
   - **Attachment** (optional) — Upload instruction file
4. Click **Save**

#### Grading Submissions

1. Navigate to **Submissions** from the sidebar
2. View student submissions for each assignment
3. For each submission:
   - Download and review the student's file
   - Enter a **grade** (out of max grade)
   - Provide **feedback**
   - Click **Save Grade**

### 3.3 Entering Exam Results

1. Navigate to **Grades** from the sidebar
2. Select the exam
3. For each student:
   - Enter **marks obtained**
   - System auto-calculates **grade** and **grade points**
   - Add optional **remarks**
4. Click **Save All**

### 3.4 Creating Announcements

1. Navigate to **Announcements** from the sidebar
2. Click **"New Announcement"**
3. Fill in:
   - **Title** — Announcement heading
   - **Message** — Full announcement text
   - **Target** — All students, specific course, or specific group
   - **Priority** — Low, Medium, High, or Urgent
4. Click **Publish**

### 3.5 Viewing Your Timetable

1. Navigate to **Timetable** from the sidebar
2. View your weekly teaching schedule
3. See room assignments and class times

## 4. Administrator Guide

### 4.1 Dashboard

The admin dashboard provides system-wide overview:
- **Total students** — Active, pending, and suspended counts
- **Fee collection** — Total payments received
- **Pending receipts** — Receipts awaiting review
- **System alerts** — Critical notifications

### 4.2 Student Management

#### Approving New Students

1. Navigate to **Students** from the sidebar
2. Filter by status: **Pending**
3. Click on a student to review their details
4. Assign:
   - **Course** — Select from available courses
   - **Study mode** — Day, Evening, or Weekend
   - **Registration number** — Assign a unique reg number
5. Change status to **Active**
6. Click **Save** — Fee balance auto-calculates

#### Bulk Operations

- Use the search and filter tools to find students
- Sort by name, course, status, or registration number

### 4.3 Fee Management

#### Configuring Fee Items

1. Navigate to **Fees** from the sidebar
2. View existing fee items
3. To add a new fee:
   - **Name** — e.g., "Library Fee"
   - **Amount** — in UGX
   - **Category** — General, Tuition, Functional
   - **Frequency** — Once, Yearly, Per Semester, Per Term
   - **Applies to** — All, Diploma Only, Semester Basis, Term Basis
   - **Optional?** — Yes (student can opt out) or No (mandatory)

#### Reviewing Receipts

1. Navigate to **Receipts** from the sidebar
2. View receipts by status: Processing, Verified, Review Required, Rejected
3. For receipts needing review:
   - View the original receipt image
   - Compare AI-extracted data with receipt
   - **Approve** — Creates payment record, recalculates balance
   - **Reject** — Add rejection reason

### 4.4 Clearance Management

1. Navigate to **Clearance** from the sidebar
2. View all clearance requests with status summary
3. Click a request to see its 4 steps
4. For each pending step:
   - Click **Review**
   - Add notes (required for rejection)
   - **Approve** or **Reject**
5. When all 4 steps are approved, the request automatically moves to **Cleared**

### 4.5 Course & Department Setup

#### Creating a Department

1. Navigate to **Courses** from the sidebar
2. Click **Departments** tab
3. Click **"Add Department"**
4. Enter department name and save

#### Creating a Course

1. Navigate to **Courses** from the sidebar
2. Click **"Add Course"**
3. Fill in all fields:
   - Course code, name, department
   - Program level and duration
   - Tuition rates (Day, Evening, Weekend)
   - Max capacity and entry requirements
4. Click **Save**

### 4.6 Timetable & Scheduling

1. Navigate to **Scheduling** from the sidebar
2. Create recurring schedules:
   - Select course, lecturer, day of week
   - Set start/end times and room
   - System checks for **conflicts** automatically
3. Navigate to **Timetable** to manage individual entries

### 4.7 Exam Management

1. Navigate to **Exams** from the sidebar
2. Create exams with date, time, venue, and course
3. View results entered by lecturers

### 4.8 Announcements

1. Navigate to **Announcements** from the sidebar
2. Create system-wide or targeted announcements
3. Set priority levels for urgent notices

### 4.9 Academic Calendar

1. Navigate to **Calendar** from the sidebar
2. Add events: semester start/end, holidays, exam periods
3. Events are visible to all users

### 4.10 Reports & Analytics

1. Navigate to **Reports** from the sidebar
2. View enrollment statistics, fee collection reports, and attendance summaries

### 4.11 Audit Logs

1. Navigate to **Audit Logs** from the sidebar
2. View all administrative actions:
   - Who did what, when
   - Before/after values for changes
3. Filter by user, action type, or date range

### 4.12 System Settings

1. Navigate to **Settings** from the sidebar
2. Configure:
   - Institution name and details
   - Academic year and semester
   - Financial settings (currency, etc.)
   - Admin password management

## 6. FAQ

**Q: I signed up but can't access my dashboard. Why?**  
A: New student accounts require admin approval. Contact your institution's admin office.

**Q: My receipt was rejected. What should I do?**  
A: Check the rejection reason in your fee page. Common reasons:
- Receipt image is blurry or not a genuine SchoolPay receipt
- Name on receipt doesn't match your profile
- Transaction code was already used
Contact admin if you believe the rejection is incorrect.

**Q: How do I know my fee balance is correct?**  
A: Your fee balance is calculated automatically based on your course tuition + mandatory fees + optional fees − approved payments. View the full breakdown in the Fees section.

**Q: Can I access the system on my phone?**  
A: Yes! The system is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers.

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive a reset link via email.

## 7. Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|---|---|
| Can't log in | Verify email is confirmed; check password; clear browser cache |
| Page loads slowly | Check internet connection; try a different browser |
| Receipt upload fails | Ensure file is < 50MB; use JPEG, PNG, or PDF format |
| Missing course data | Contact admin to verify your enrollment status |
| Grade not showing | Grades appear after the lecturer enters them |
| Clearance stuck | Contact the office for the pending step (Finance, Library, etc.) |
| Error messages | Take a screenshot and send to support@tech4lyf.com |
